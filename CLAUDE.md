# Welkin Technohub PVT.LTD — Company Website

## What This Website Does

This is a **single-page marketing/brochure website** for **Welkin Technohub PVT.LTD**, an IT services company based in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India.

## Purpose

- Presents the company's services and technology expertise to potential clients
- Provides a contact/inquiry form for leads and job applicants
- Doubles as a **career page** — the same contact form handles both client inquiries and job applications

## Sections

| Section | Description |
|---|---|
| **Hero** | Tagline: "Take your Business Online with Welkin Technohub PVT.LTD" |
| **About Us** | Company philosophy — custom solutions, long-term client relationships |
| **Services** | Web Development, App Development, UI/UX Design, QA Services |
| **Technologies** | Filterable grid: PHP, .Net, iOS, Android, Angular.js, React.js, HTML5, MongoDB, MySQL, MsSQL, Oracle, WordPress |
| **Contact Us / Career** | Address, email, phone, Google Maps embed, and a PHP-backed contact form |
| **Footer** | Address, useful links, services list, social media (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn) |

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** Plain HTML5, Bootstrap 4, jQuery, AOS (scroll animations), Isotope (portfolio filtering), VenoBox (lightbox), Owl Carousel
- **Backend:** `forms/contact.php` handles form submissions (PHP mailer)
- **Fonts:** Google Fonts (Open Sans, Raleway)
- **Icons:** IcoFont, Boxicons
